## अप्पय्य-दीक्षित-जयन्ती #५०२

Observed on Kṛṣṇa-Prathamā tithi of Kanyā (sidereal solar) month (Prātaḥ/paraviddha). The event has been commemorated since it occurred in 4620 (kali era).
