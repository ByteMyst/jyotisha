## मासशिवरात्रिः

Observed on Kṛṣṇa-Caturdaśī tithi of every (lunar) month (Niśīthaḥ/paraviddha). Monthly Shivaratri day.

## सर्वनदी-रजस्वला

Observed on day 3 of Kaṭakaḥ (sidereal solar) month (Sūryodayaḥ/puurvaviddha). Do not take bath in any river during these days.
