## तिन्दुकाष्टमी

Observed on Kṛṣṇa-Aṣṭamī tithi of Jyaiṣṭhaḥ (lunar) month (Sūryodayaḥ/puurvaviddha). Start of one year vratam of monthly shiva pooja as per chaturvaga chintamani.
