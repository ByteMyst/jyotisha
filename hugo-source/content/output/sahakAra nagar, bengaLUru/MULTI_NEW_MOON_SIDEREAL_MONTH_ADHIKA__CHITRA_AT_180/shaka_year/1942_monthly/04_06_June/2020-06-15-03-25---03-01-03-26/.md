## षडशीति-मिथुनरविः

Observed on day 1 of Mithunam (sidereal solar) month (Sūryodayaḥ/puurvaviddha). Perform danam of clothes, food and water.

वस्त्रान्नपानदानानि मिथुने विहितानि तु॥
