## कंस-वधः

Observed on Śukla-Daśamī tithi of Kārttikaḥ (lunar) month (Sūryodayaḥ/puurvaviddha). Lord Krishna killed Kamsa on this day; most popularly celebrated in Mathura and Uttar Pradesh.
